Painting Masterclass
Learning the basics of art composition, working with form, color, and light, and meeting new friends
Modern painting masterclass for beginners, where you can learn the basics in 4 hours, individually make a picture, and get acquainted with interesting people. All required materials are included in the cost of the masterclass.
How It Works
During the masterclass, you will learn the basics of painting theory and create your own picture
Theoretical part
During the masterclass, we will paint a small still-life. At the beginning of the masterclass, the teacher will talk about the basics of composition, the use of color, and painting techniques.
Painting
You will paint a still-life with acrylic paints. If necessary, the teacher will advise you and help build the composition and work with the form and color.
New acquaintances and communication
At our masterclasses, there is always a friendly social atmosphere with various interesting and creative people, who can inspire with their creative and life experiences.

FAQ
If you have any questions, you can ask them by email hello@company.com or by phone +44 1234 567 890
Does it make sense to come if I don't know how to draw at all?
Yes, the masterclass program has been designed for beginners, you will draw an uncomplicated still-life, and the teacher will help you and advise you if something doesn't work out.
What materials will we be using for painting?
During the masterclass, we will be using acrylics, as they dry the fastest. You can take your work home straight after the class. We will be painting on a 30x45cm canvas.
How long does the lesson last, will we have time to finish the picture?
The lesson lasts 4 hours. Since the still-life consists of very few details, and the size of the canvas is small, you can easily finish it during this time.
